Our Mission

What we do and how we do it.

We connect students with school and community leaders. We work with student leaders to distribute surveys, analyze the data, and present results directly to decision-makers who shape your city's school policies.

We help schools improve by making sure student voices are heard and genuinely acted upon.

📋
Survey & Research
Large-scale surveys designed and distributed by student leaders to capture authentic peer perspectives at scale.
📊
Data Analysis
We help students turn raw responses into clear, compelling insights that decision-makers can't ignore.
🏛️
Policy Advocacy
Results are presented directly to principals, school boards, and city officials to drive real, lasting change.
🌐
Global Collaboration
Chapters across cities and countries share knowledge, strategies, and wins with one another.
Meet Our Founder

Ayra Dhillon

Ayra founded YHAN after seeing how rarely student perspectives are reflected in school rules and policies. Watching decisions being made about students, without students, she knew something had to change.

Through large-scale surveys and direct collaboration with cities, she works to translate student voice into school policy, building a global network of young advocates who refuse to be ignored.
